The Photographer

Telling Stories
Through Sport

I believe every athlete carries a story — in the flex of a muscle, the grit of a final lap, the silent moment before the whistle. My job is to find that story and make it undeniable.

Based in Florida, I work with professional teams, collegiate programs, and individual athletes across the country. My images have appeared in Sports Illustrated, ESPN Magazine, and dozens of regional publications.

Whether it's sideline coverage of a championship game or an intimate portrait session, I bring the same obsessive attention to light, timing, and emotion to every assignment.
